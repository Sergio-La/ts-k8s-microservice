1. Verifica el Deployment y los Pods:
  kubectl get deployment --namespace {{ .Release.Namespace }} {{ include "mi-servicio-ts.fullname" . }}
  kubectl get pods --namespace {{ .Release.Namespace }} -l "app.kubernetes.io/instance={{ .Release.Name }}"

2. El Service es de tipo ClusterIP (puerto {{ .Values.service.port }} -> {{ .Values.containerPort }}).
   Para probarlo desde dentro del clúster:
  kubectl --namespace {{ .Release.Namespace }} port-forward svc/{{ include "mi-servicio-ts.fullname" . }} 8080:{{ .Values.service.port }}
  curl http://127.0.0.1:8080/health
  curl http://127.0.0.1:8080/api/v1/hello
